Center for Sports Development


Forming that integral individual which is the main thrust of the vision of the University means that we also want to develop the physique and the athletic abilities of our students. Providing the proper coaches, the equipment and facilities, as well as the opportunities for the students to excel in both sports and academic endeavors and activities, the Athletics Department ensures that each student and/or athlete excels in his or her chosen field.

The Athletics Department upholds the competitive spirit of LPU Cavite. By fostering healthy competitions and harvesting great life lessons from both victory and defeat, the department allows the spirit of the University to soar while imbibing in the athlete the many life lessons about prevailing in life through sports.
